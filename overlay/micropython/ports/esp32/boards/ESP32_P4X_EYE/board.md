The following firmware is applicable to the ESP32-P4X-EYE vision development
board based on ESP32-P4.

This board must be equipped with at least 16 MiB external SPI Flash and PSRAM.
The default configuration enables the ESP32-P4 target, the ESP32-P4X-EYE flash
layout, external ESP32-C6 hosted WiFi/BLE wiring, and the board-specific camera
baseline used by ESP-VISION.
